End-to-end system comparison on 10 held-out utterances (44.1 kHz). GT: ground-truth recording  ·  DiffSinger: full-size baseline  ·  DiffSinger-Compact: reduced-backbone baseline  ·  Latent2Mel: latent → mel ablation  ·  VISinger 2: state-of-the-art reference  ·  LiHFSinger: proposed system.
Vocoder ablation on 15 utterances reconstructed from ground-truth mel/STFT features. GT: original recording  ·  HiFi-GAN / Vocos: standard neural vocoders  ·  LiHF: proposed dual-branch vocoder (latent input)  ·  LiHF (no WORLD): without WORLD-branch  ·  LiHF (mel): mel-input variant  ·  LiHF (mel, no WORLD): mel + no WORLD  ·  LiHF (full mel): full-resolution mel variant.
